Common Refrigerator Issues

Not Cooling Properly

Food not staying cold or inconsistent temperature levels.

Excessive Frost

Heavy frost buildup in freezer due to defrost system faults.

Strange Noises

Buzzing, clicking, or rattling noises from the unit.

Water Leakage

Water pooling beneath or inside the refrigerator.

Ice Maker Not Working

Ice not forming or dispensing properly.

Running Constantly

Compressor or fans never shut off, increasing energy use.

Foul Odors

Unpleasant smells from spoiled food or mold buildup.

Temperature Fluctuations

Intermittent warming or freezing of stored items.

Door Seal Problems

Worn gasket or improper sealing leading to air leaks.

Control Panel Malfunction

Buttons or display not responding correctly.
