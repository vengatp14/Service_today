Privacy Policy

At Service Today, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you access our appliance service solutions.

1. Introduction

Your trust is our top priority. We respect your privacy and are transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices and your rights regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide smooth and efficient service experiences. This includes:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Your full name and contact number
  • Email address for communication
  • Location/address for service visits
  • Details about your appliance and service request

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device/browser information for website access
  • Log files and session data
  • Usage patterns and interaction with our site

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and provide appliance services at your location
  • To update you on service status and technician visits
  • To improve our service offerings and customer support
  • To resolve complaints and technical queries

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information in these scenarios:

4.1 With Your Consent

If you authorize us to share your information (e.g., for third-party repair specialists).

4.2 Legal Requirements

  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement
  • To enforce our Terms & Conditions
  • To protect the safety of our users and services

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented robust safeguards:

  • SSL encryption for all website transactions
  • Restricted access to customer data internally
  • Regular security audits and protection protocols

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
