Common Television Issues

TV Not Turning On

Completely unresponsive when pressing power button or using remote.

No Picture or Sound

Black screen with no audio, even though TV is powered on.

Distorted Picture

Blurry, pixelated, or fuzzy images disrupting viewing experience.

Audio Problems

Low volume, no sound, or distorted audio output from speakers.

Remote Not Working

Buttons unresponsive or remote not syncing with the TV.

Screen Flickering

Flashing screen or horizontal/vertical lines disrupting visuals.

TV Overheating

Excessive heat causing performance issues or auto shut-down.

Connectivity Problems

External devices like HDMI, USB, or gaming consoles not connecting.

Smart TV App Issues

Streaming apps freezing, not loading, or crashing frequently.

Mount or Stand Issues

TV stand unstable or wall mount improperly secured or loose.
